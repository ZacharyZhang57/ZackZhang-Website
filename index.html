About Me

I am a Creative Director and Choreographer currently based in Los Angeles. I specalized in creating projects that utilize movement and dance

I grew up moving in between utah and texas then landing in LA after college. during college I found my love for dance and creation of any kind of art. After moving to LA then working and training as a dancer I have equipped myself with artistic and technical knowledge on how to execute the visuals I have always wanted to since I even started dance.

My Vision

Creatively my approach is always a music first approach. To me the music is a perfect guide to even what the story is in the context of any visual or project. my biggest strenght in a project is my ability to create tension with a solid sense of progression and storytelling that not only makes sense within the context of the story but also movement-wise aswell.

When it comes to execution, attention to detail and leadership abilities is what drives me to create quality products efficiently. On a set I am able to delegate tasks, give simple and concise direction while upkeeping a fun and lighthearted enviornment!
